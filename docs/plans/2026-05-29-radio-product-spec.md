# Tiko Radio — Product Specification

> Saved from user-provided spec. Source of truth for product direction.

## Purpose

Tiko Radio is a safe, parent-controlled audio environment for children.

It allows parents to create collections of trusted audio content from multiple sources and present them through an extremely simple interface that children can use independently.

Tiko Radio is designed to remove distractions, advertisements, recommendations, comments, and complex navigation, allowing children to focus entirely on listening.

---

## Vision

Communication, learning, comfort, and entertainment should be accessible through a simple and safe audio experience.

Tiko Radio provides children with access to approved stories, songs, educational content, family recordings, and other audio without exposing them to the complexity of traditional media platforms.

---

## Target Audience

**Primary**
- Children aged 2–12
- Children with communication difficulties
- Autistic children
- Children requiring simplified interfaces

**Secondary**
- Parents, guardians, teachers, speech therapists, caregivers

---

## Core Principles

### Parent Controlled
- Parents decide exactly which content is available.
- Children cannot search, browse, add, or access unapproved content.

### Child First
- Interface understandable within seconds.
- Reading should not be required.
- Large touch targets throughout.
- Navigation predictable and consistent.

### Content Agnostic
- Not tied to a specific content provider.
- Sources: Tiko Library, Parent Uploads, YouTube Links, Podcasts, Audiobooks, Family Recordings, Educational Resources.
- The child should never need to know where content originates.

### Safe by Design
- Children never encounter: ads, comments, recommendations, related content, social features, external links.

---

## Content Sources

### Tiko Library
- Curated content: stories, songs, learning, public domain, educational audio.
- May support offline playback.

### Parent Uploads
- MP3, audiobooks, stories, recorded messages, family recordings.
- May support offline playback.

### YouTube
- Parents add individual videos or playlists.
- Only metadata stored: video ID, title, thumbnail, duration.
- Media streamed directly from YouTube.
- **No YouTube audio/video stored, downloaded, or redistributed by Tiko.**

---

## Child Experience

### Home Screen
- Displays collections as large visual tiles (Stories, Bedtime, Songs, Learning, Favourite, etc.).

### Collection Screen
- Parent-approved content with thumbnail, artwork, optional title.
- Tap to begin playback.

### Playback Screen
- Artwork, Play, Pause, Previous, Next.
- Optional progress indicator.
- Clean, distraction-free. No comments, recommendations, external content.

### Radio Mode
- Continuous playback from a collection.
- One choice → listening continues automatically.
- Example: Bedtime Radio → story → story → lullaby → story → calm music.
- Continues until collection finishes or stopped.

### Shuffle Mode
- Optional setting. Random playback order.

### Repeat Mode
- Optional setting. Collection repeats indefinitely.

---

## Parent Experience

### Parent Area
- Protected by PIN, biometric, or device authentication.

### Content Management
- Add, remove, reorder, rename content.
- Create and delete collections.
- Set collection artwork.
- Add content to multiple collections.

### Child Profiles *(future)*
- Separate collections, settings, listening history per child.

---

## Accessibility
- Screen readers, large touch targets, high contrast, reduced motion, VoiceOver, TalkBack.
- Navigation usable for non-readers.

---

## Offline Support
- **Allowed:** Tiko Library, parent uploads, family recordings.
- **Not allowed:** Downloading YouTube video/audio.

---

## Future Features
- **Family Radio** — stations from family member recordings.
- **Smart Playlists** — based on preferences, frequency, time of day.
- **Sleep Mode** — playback duration, fade-out timer, bedtime schedules.
- **Podcast Support** — subscribe to approved podcasts, add episodes to collections.
- **Audiobook Support** — collections with resume listening progress.

---

## Success Criteria

A child can:
- Open Tiko Radio.
- Recognize a collection.
- Press a single button.
- Listen independently.
- Never encounter unsafe or distracting content.

A parent can:
- Add content in under 30 seconds.
- Fully control what the child can access.
- Create a safe audio environment tailored to their child.
- Trust that no unexpected content will appear.
